# Partners and Contributors

The Oracle AI Developers Community thrives on collaboration and the invaluable contributions of its partners and individual developers. This section highlights key partners who are working alongside Oracle to advance AI innovation and the broader ecosystem of contributors.

## Strategic Partners

Oracle actively collaborates with a diverse range of partners to extend the reach and capabilities of its AI offerings. These partnerships are crucial for fostering innovation and providing comprehensive solutions to developers and enterprises.

*   **Datacules LLC**: A prominent Database Managed Expert Services Provider and an Oracle PartnerNetwork (OPN) Certified entity. Datacules LLC plays a significant role in the Oracle ecosystem, offering specialized expertise and services that complement Oracle AI Database solutions [1, 2, 3].

*   **Cloud Providers**: Oracle is committed to multicloud strategies, partnering with major cloud providers to ensure flexibility and broad accessibility for its AI Database. This includes collaborations with:
    *   **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**: Partners can offer Oracle Database@AWS to customers through the AWS Channel Partner Private Offers (CPPO) program [4].
    *   **Google Cloud**: Oracle Database@Google Cloud integrates Oracle AI Database services, including secure, natural language access to Oracle data through Gemini Enterprise [5, 6].
    *   **Microsoft Azure**: Oracle's strategic partnership with Microsoft extends to various cloud and AI initiatives.

*   **Oracle Defense Ecosystem**: Established in June 2025, this ecosystem brings together leading startups, scale-ups, and established enterprises to drive AI and data innovation specifically for defense applications [7].

## Community Contributors

The strength of the Oracle AI Developers Community also comes from its individual contributors—developers, data scientists, and AI enthusiasts who share their knowledge, build projects, and provide feedback. We encourage active participation through:

*   **Code Contributions**: Submitting code, examples, and tools that leverage Oracle AI Database features.
*   **Documentation**: Improving and expanding the technical documentation to help fellow developers.
*   **Forum Participation**: Engaging in discussions, answering questions, and sharing insights on platforms like the Oracle Developer Community forums and social media.
*   **Event Engagement**: Participating in Oracle AI World Tour events, hackathons, and workshops to showcase projects and learn from peers.

## Become a Partner or Contributor

We invite organizations and individuals to join our growing ecosystem. Whether through formal partnerships or community contributions, your involvement helps shape the future of AI with Oracle. For partnership inquiries, please visit the [Oracle PartnerNetwork (OPN) program](https://www.oracle.com/partner/).
