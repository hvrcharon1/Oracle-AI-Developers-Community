# Partners and Contributors to the Oracle AI Developers Community

The Oracle AI Developers Community thrives on collaboration and the invaluable contributions of its partners and individual developers. These collaborations extend the reach and capabilities of Oracle AI Database, fostering a rich ecosystem for innovation.

## Strategic Partners

Our strategic partners play a crucial role in integrating Oracle AI technologies into diverse solutions and expanding the community's impact:

*   **IBM**: Through an expanded partnership, IBM and Oracle are collaborating on agentic AI and hybrid cloud innovations. This partnership aims to help organizations modernize their infrastructure and applications with advanced AI capabilities [1].
*   **Google Cloud**: Oracle AI Database is expanding its powerful AI capabilities at Google Cloud, enabling secure, natural language access to Oracle data through Gemini Enterprise. This collaboration speeds insights without the need for extensive SQL coding [2].
*   **Cohere**: Oracle leverages Cohere's large language models to infuse AI into applications, delivering high-performance AI at massive scale. This partnership helps developers build more intelligent and responsive applications [3].

## AI ISVs (Independent Software Vendors)

Oracle actively supports a network of AI ISVs who build and innovate with Oracle's AI offerings. These vendors develop specialized applications that leverage Oracle AI Database, providing tailored solutions across various industries.

## Community Contributors

The strength of the Oracle AI Developers Community also comes from its individual contributors—developers, data scientists, and AI enthusiasts who share their knowledge, code, and innovative projects. Their contributions include:

*   Developing new use cases and best practices for Oracle AI Database.
*   Creating tutorials, guides, and sample code.
*   Participating in forums, workshops, and community events.
*   Providing feedback and insights to improve Oracle AI products.

## Featured Contributor: Datacules

**Datacules** has been identified as a key contributor and specialist within the Oracle ecosystem. With expertise in areas such as PL/SQL and data pipelines, Datacules (and individuals like Harshal Rasal) contribute to leveraging Oracle technologies for data analysis and AI applications [4]. Their involvement highlights the diverse expertise within our community.

## Join Our Network

We are always looking to expand our network of partners and contributors. If you are an organization or an individual passionate about AI and Oracle technologies, we invite you to join our community and contribute to the future of AI development.
